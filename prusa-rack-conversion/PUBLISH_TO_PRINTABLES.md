# How to Publish to Printables

## When Ready
After printing and assembling the brackets, take a few photos of the installed setup, then follow these steps.

## Upload Steps

1. **Go to:** https://www.printables.com/model/new

2. **Upload files** (drag & drop from `printables/` folder):
   - `top_bracket_left_v3.stl`
   - `top_bracket_right_v3.stl`
   - `bottom_bracket_left_v3.stl`
   - `bottom_bracket_right_v3.stl`
   - Your installation photos (add these first - they get the most engagement)

3. **Title:** `Prusa MK3S/MK4S Frame to 6U Rack Mount Brackets`

4. **Copy description** from `printables/PRINTABLES_SUBMISSION.md`

5. **Print settings:**
   - Material: PETG or ABS
   - Layer height: 0.2mm
   - Infill: 40-50%
   - Supports: None

6. **Categories:** 3D Printer Accessories â†’ Prusa

7. **Tags:** `prusa, mk3s, mk4s, rack mount, 6U, server rack, bracket`

8. **License:** GNU GPL v3.0

9. **Optional source files:**
   - `BracketsV3.FCStd`
   - `prusa_rack_brackets.py`

10. **Publish!**

## Files Location
All upload files are in: `prusa-rack-conversion/printables/`
